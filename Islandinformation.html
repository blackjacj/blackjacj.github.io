Lodging

Bungalo on the beach

Enjoy your stay on Taniti by booking a bungalow overlooking the ocean right on the beach. These bungalows are small with a single room, 2 beds, and 1 bathroom. They are typically booked by honeymoons or small families. This is a great option for those that want to be cost effective.

$250 per night

Seaside Resort

Experience true serenity and comfort with Seaside Resort, a large, 4-star resort along the coast. Amenities include a large pool, gym, and spa. Many different rooms are available for rent From large 4 bedroom suites, to smaller 1 bedroom rooms. This is a once in a lifetime option for a true island experience!

$1000 per night

Family Hotel

These medium sized homes are a great option for those who are interested in exploring the forests of Taniti. Each home has two floors with 3 bedrooms and 2 bathrooms.

$500 per night

Transportation

To the island

Almost all visitors arrive to Taniti by air, though some arrive on a small cruise ship that docks in Yellow Leaf Bay for one night per week. Taniti is served by a small airport that can accommodate small jets and propeller planes. Taniti is in the process of expanding the airport so larger jets will be able to land on the island within the next few years.

Ground Transportation

Public buses serve Taniti City and run from 5 a.m. to 11 p.m. every day. Private buses serve the rest of the island. Taxis are available in Taniti City, and rental cars can be rented from a local rental agency near the airport. Bikes and helmets are available to rent from several vendors (helmets are required by law). Taniti City is fairly flat and very walkable . Many tourists stay in the area surrounding Merriton Landing: this area is easy to explore on foot.

Entertainment

Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and to visit the volcano. However, there are other things to do. Many of these activities are located in Merriton Landing, which is a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

Outdoor Activities

- zip-lining in the rainforest

- a nine-hole golf course

- going on chartered fishing tours

- taking helicopter rides

- Snorkeling

Indoor Activities

- visiting several pubs including a microbrewery

- local history museum

- dancing at a new dance club

- playing at an arcade

- visiting art galleries

- seeing a movie

Sightseeing

Taniti City

Explore the beautiful city of Taniti. This city is rich with ancient history and culture, full of many modern day activities along with museums celebrating the culture of Taniti. The city is nearby beautiful white, sandy beaches that encircle Yellow Leaf Bay.

Island Tours

Take a tour of the island via boat, bus or helicopter! This is the best way to see the island in it's entirety.

Hiking

Are you a fan of physical activity and have a sense of adventure? There are many hikes throughout the island of Taniti. Ranging from short, beginner hikes to longer, advanced hikes for those looking for more of a challenge.

Volcano

The volcano located in the rainforest is one of our most popular tourist attractions! It is a small active volcano with a hiking trail that leads to the base of the volcano.
